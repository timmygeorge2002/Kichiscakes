Our cake shop is based in Banbury, UK. We are your one stop shop in Banbury for freshly baked goodies, cakes and catering. At Kichis cakes we use only fresh and wholesome ingredients to provide you with nothing but the best.

Our perfect desserts would complement any event of yours. We also cater for Sandwich buffets, Finger buffets and Tea breaks. We make custom shape cakes as well. We hope to grow with the inspiration our customers give us. Looking forward to serve you!
